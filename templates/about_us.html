About us

We are focUS, a team committed to implementing A.I. to create a time-efficient solution that helps you find your next volunteering endeavor.

Here is the team

We are a group passionate about streamlining your volunteer search as we ourselves can resonate with how the lack of proper resources and the weight of unstructured individual research slows down the volunteer search process. We also noticed there to be discrepancies that disproportionately favour well-known organizations as they have pre-established resources and connections within a community. We hope to bring exposure to smaller volunteer organizations and provide equal recognition and opportunity to volunteers across Canada.
