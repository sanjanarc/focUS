    • About the organization

    To honour the diverse communities of our City, the Mayor's Office of Special Projects and Community Events (MOSPCE) organizes, manages and directs Mayoral public ceremonies, celebrations and receptions, in partnership with the city agencies and support of the private sector. These special events are hosted at Gracie Mansion—also known as the People's House—and other iconic sites across the city. MOSPCE is associated with various communities within NYC including Asian-Americans Immigrants, refugees, LGBTQIA+, low-income populations, Military/ veteran families etc.
